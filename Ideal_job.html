The advertisement

This position is an internal consulting style position, meaning that you need to be active across multiple projects understanding the needs of multiple disciplines to lift the capacity and adoption rates of both technology and business teams. Strategic thinking and human centred design knowledge is important to ensure that the technology team understand, build and deploy the products needed to truly uplift the business and that the business stakeholders fund and adopting them appropriately. This position appeals as it would take full advantage of the range of skills and experience I have acquired over time, and it would always be involved with the cutting edge of technology and product design in the business.

Skills, qualifications and experience required

The position requires strategic thinking and leadership skills, as well as a broad knowledge of technology roles and the state of IT. It requires the ability to look at raw technology with a vision of how it could be deployed in the business and a realistic knowledge of the time and effort that will require. It requires human design skills to ensure products are built for optimal use by users and customers and strong communication skills at all levels.

A plan to obtain the necessary attributes

A formal IT qualification leaning towards management skills, security, cloud and innovation would help to gain credibility with the IT stakeholders. I will progress via OUA or switch to a Graduate Certificate in IT Management. I have already completed courses in project management and service design. Networking and digital innovation conferences are important to stay aware of the latest developments in technology and the digital workplace. I attend the digital expos for state and national government each year as well as events including Salesforce, Microsoft and ServiceNow.
